About Me

Hello, I am Naman Krishna Pande, a researcher in the field of mathematics, specialising in the modelling of physical phenomena using machine learning. I am keenly interested in the development of deep learning techniques and in integrating them with the underlying physics of real-world systems.

My work focuses on building models that not only predict outcomes accurately but also respect the inherent structure and dynamics of the phenomena being studied. I enjoy exploring areas such as physics-informed neural networks, numerical simulations, and stochastic modeling, aiming to bridge the gap between theoretical mathematics and practical applications.

Beyond research, I am passionate about sharing knowledge, mentoring aspiring learners, and contributing to collaborative projects that advance both mathematics and artificial intelligence. My goal is to develop solutions that are interpretable, efficient, and impactful, helping translate complex scientific concepts into actionable insights.
